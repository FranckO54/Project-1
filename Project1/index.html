Comunity Details

Who are we?

We are a Interior community club, Specialize in interior Designing habitable space in a home or at a workspace area and etc. Based in Woolwich we are a friendly bunch who simply love the structuring the design of homes or workspace to meet client needs. We focus strictly on meeting the needs and requirments of our clients to. To give them the satisfaction they want to feel when they enter their house.

What do we do?

As a community, we provide plans and sketches of the clients request, as well as some running some test in a Live 3D platform to give our clients sense of security and clarity on how the inside of the house would look like. We base our projects on the clients need and work to colloborate effectively with our clients.
